How a Moen job works

A Moen job moves through three phases and eight stages. Press Start to walk through each one.

Select Start to begin. Each stage will reveal one at a time — select Next to continue through the full lifecycle.

Phase 1 — Customer

Lead generated

Moen sends a customer who has purchased a Moen Flo device and needs installation. The Ops team receives the customer's details.

Customer outreach

The Ops team contacts the customer to confirm their details and collect three preferred scheduling dates and times.

Qualification

The agent walks through the qualification criteria — property type, smartphone, shutoff valve, pipe material, Wi-Fi, and electrical outlet — to confirm the installation is feasible.

Phase 2 — Matching

Pro matching

The Ops team contacts eligible pros via email and SMS simultaneously (automated claiming). The first pro to reply claims the job. If no pro claims within 6 hours, the team calls pros individually from the list (manual calling).

Assignment

The job is assigned to the claiming pro via the Admin panel using the pro's business PK. Both the pro and the customer receive a confirmation email.

Phase 3 — Installation & Close

Install

The pro completes the installation and submits before-and-after photos and an invoice to the Ops team. The Ops team checks that the photo matches the invoiced install type.

Payment

The Ops team verifies the install type, updates the payout amount in Admin, and triggers payment to the pro. Payment is deposited within 3–5 business days.

Completion

The job is marked complete in Admin. A completion email goes to Moen. A follow-up survey goes to the customer. If no response within 72 hours, the job is still marked complete.

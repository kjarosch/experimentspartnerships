The Ops team sends a job offer email and SMS to all eligible pros at the same time using the Google Groups account. Only job details and zip code are shared — no customer PII at this stage.

Waiting for a claim

If a pro replies via email or SMS within 6 hours, they claim the job. All other pros are immediately notified the job is no longer available. If no one replies within 6 hours, the team moves to manual calling.
All other pros notified when claimed
Step 2 — Manual calling (fallback)
Team calls pros individually
Select to expand
If no pro claims within 6 hours, the Ops team begins calling pros from the list one by one. Share only job details and zip code — no customer PII. Ask if they want to claim and confirm a timeframe.
First to accept on the phone claims the job
Fallback — no pro available
Job referred back to Moen
Select to expand
If no pro is found after exhausting all options including Moen's Pro Finder Tool, the job is referred back to Moen. The customer is informed they will need to hire their own licensed plumber. Moen reimburses the customer directly.
